about us

Commited to quality

Barkley Auto-fix runs out of a specially constructed workshop but mostly mobile with all the necessary tools that a dealership would have, along with diagnostic tools specific to your car model, which are crucial for an accurate diagnosis.

We specialize in Audi vehicles mainly A4,A5,A6,A7,Q5, Mercedes Benz and Volkswagen. All kinds of repairs are done here including B8, 5 Electronic steering rack repair, fitment and programming of motor vehicles computer programs. All of this is done through our good code of practice.

Accountability, Integrity and Transparency

Because of our expertise, broad skill set, specialized understanding of German vehicles, and exceptionally high level of customer service, we have established a solid reputation throughout the province. We only allow the best work to leave our workshop since your satisfaction is important to us.

Our reputation is built on knowledge, excellent work, reliability, and client pleasure
